# Persona — Healthcare Champion (problem owner / entry point)

## Who
Director/VP of Security & Safety or Chief Security Officer; Director of Emergency Management; **Workplace Violence Program Coordinator** (a new, mandate-driven role); plus the CNO as clinical sponsor. This person owns the WPV problem but usually does NOT hold the budget (that's the EDM).

## What they care about
Protecting staff (especially ED + behavioral units, where assaults cluster), meeting the mandate/Joint Commission standard, faster response, reducing incidents, and not being blamed after an event.

## Where to find them (the hard role)
- **IAHSS** (International Association for Healthcare Security & Safety) member/speaker lists — the goldmine for this otherwise-invisible role.
- LinkedIn (title filters: Security, Emergency Management, Workplace Violence).
- The person who POSTED the security/EM job opening (job-board signal).

## How to engage
Lead with the specific firing signal (mandate deadline/enforcement, recent incident, OSHA citation). One-off, human-written, via LinkedIn or physical mail (firewall-safe channels). Arm them to sell the EDM internally; also map a direct path to the EDM.

## Entry → EDM motion
Champion ≠ EDM almost always. Enter through the reachable, motivated champion; co-sell up to the COO/CFO/CNO; QSO clears Gravity only when EDM access is evidenced.
