# Persona — Healthcare Economic Decision Maker (EDM)

## Who
COO or CFO for an enterprise safety platform; at system level a VP of Security / Support Services; capital committee or board for large spend. **CNO (Chief Nursing Officer) is the clinical co-sponsor and the unlock** — staff safety drives nurse retention, which is a CNO/CFO priority.

## What they care about
Liability and workers'-comp cost, nurse turnover, Joint Commission / mandate compliance, capital efficiency, avoiding a publicized failed-response incident. They buy on outcome and risk, not features.

## Where to find them (firewall-safe)
- **IRS Form 990** (nonprofit hospitals): lists officers + comp → names CEO/CFO/COO directly. (ProPublica Nonprofit Explorer.)
- Hospital "leadership/about" pages (crawl).
- Public hospital district board minutes.
- MSRB EMMA bond official statements (capital plans).

## How to reach
Champion-led first (security/EM director brings it in), then multithread to the EDM. QSO requires evidence of EDM access, not just the champion. Note: security often reports under Facilities/Support Services — map that chain.

## Gravity signals
New CSO/Chief Safety/VP Security hire; board safety resolution; post-incident task force; funded capital plan or bond.
