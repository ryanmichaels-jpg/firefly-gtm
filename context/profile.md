# Company Profile — Firefly

## What they are
Firefly is a unified AI + IoT physical-safety platform. It pairs proprietary hardware with an AI software layer to collapse the time between a safety incident and an effective response.
- **Ember (software):** 3D digital twin of a site, AI detection/triage/response agent, direct EMS integration, mass notification, asset + people management, roll-call/mustering. Pitch: "connect tools, consolidate critical information, accelerate response."
- **Lattice (hardware):** panic buttons, gunshot detection, resilient encrypted **no-IT private-network gateway**, 5-year battery, white-glove install.
- Job-to-be-done: **Detect → Locate → Alert → Respond.**

## Mission / vision
Re-normalize safety, accelerate emergency response, unlock workforce potential. Stated vision: become "the trusted standard… infrastructure every organization needs" (Palantir-for-safety framing).

## Beachhead (this project's focus)
Healthcare = care-delivery providers (acute-care hospitals + behavioral health). The v2 build covers **all 50 states + DC, ~5,362 hospitals.** Priority for FORGE Event scoring is the in-force/upcoming mandate states. See `icp-definition.md`.

## Primary moat to reinforce
Trusted-standard / regulatory moat (certifications + validated references + embedded system-of-record + multi-year switching costs). See `positioning.md`.
