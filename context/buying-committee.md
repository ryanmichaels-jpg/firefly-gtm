# Buying Committee — all verticals (reference)

Full table in `data/reference/buying-committee.csv`. Healthcare deep-dives in `personas/`. Rule across every vertical: **Champion ≠ EDM.** Enter through the reachable champion, multithread to the budget holder; QSO clears Gravity only when EDM access is evidenced.

| Vertical | Economic Decision Maker | Champion (entry) | Key association |
|---|---|---|---|
| K-12 | School Board + Superintendent (often grant/bond funded) | Director of Safety & Security / Emergency Mgmt; District Police Chief | State school-safety offices; MOSS |
| Higher Ed | VP Admin & Finance / COO / CFO; Cabinet/Trustees | Chief of Campus Police / Public Safety / Chief Safety Officer | IACLEA |
| Healthcare | COO/CFO; system VP Security/Support Services; CNO co-sponsor | Director/VP Security & Safety; EM Director; WPV Coordinator | IAHSS |
| Hospitality | Corp VP Security/Risk/Ops; ownership group/REIT; GM (independent) | Corp Dir Security/Loss Prevention/Risk; Hotel GM | AHLA |
| Retail | VP/SVP Asset Protection or Loss Prevention → COO/CFO | VP/Dir Asset Protection / Loss Prevention | RILA AP Leaders Council; NRF; LPRC |
| Corporations | CSO / VP Global Security (reports to CEO); COO/CFO; Head of Workplace/RE | Dir Corporate/Physical Security; EH&S; Business Continuity | ASIS International |
| Government | City Manager / County Admin / Agency Head; Procurement runs RFP | Dir Emergency Management (OEM); General Services/Facilities | IAEM; NIGP |

Reachability splits public vs private: K-12, higher ed, government, and public hospital districts have named, emailable decision-makers + public board/procurement records. Private health systems, corporations, and hotel ownership lean on LinkedIn + the associations above.
